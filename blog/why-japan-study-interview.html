PRACTICAL GUIDE

Explaining why you want to study in Japan

Build a coherent answer that connects your past experience, the chosen program, and your future plan.

Start with the problem you want to solve

A strong answer begins with an academic or professional question, not only an admiration for Japanese culture.

Explain why this program

Name the curriculum, research environment, or learning opportunity that directly supports your goal. Show that you have investigated the program.

Connect the degree to your future

Describe how you will use the knowledge after graduation. The plan can evolve, but it should be concrete enough to demonstrate direction.

Make every link explicit

Your past experience should explain your interest; the program should explain your choice; your future plan should explain the value of the study.

Need personal practice?

Use this framework as a starting point, then practice follow-up questions in a realistic conversation.
