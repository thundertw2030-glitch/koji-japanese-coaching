How to structure a Japanese interview self-introduction

A practical framework for a concise, credible introduction.

What the interviewer needs

Help the interviewer understand your professional identity, relevant experience, evidence-based strength, and connection to the role. Do not repeat your entire résumé.

A four-part structure

Use this order: current field, relevant experience, one supported strength, and a brief connection to the opportunity.

Prepare the next question

For every statement, prepare the situation, your action, and the result that support it.
