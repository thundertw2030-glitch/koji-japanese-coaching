PRACTICAL GUIDE

Japanese Interview Preparation Checklist

A printable checklist for content, evidence, questions, and the online interview environment.

Before the interview

  • I can explain why this company and role are relevant to my experience.
  • I have one concrete example for each claimed strength.
  • I can explain a challenge, my action, and the outcome.
  • I have researched the organization and prepared thoughtful questions.
  • I have checked camera, microphone, lighting, background, and internet.

During practice

  • Keep the first answer concise.
  • Pause before answering difficult questions.
  • Use specific nouns and verbs instead of vague expressions.
  • Confirm the question when its meaning is unclear.

Need personal practice?

Use this framework as a starting point, then practice follow-up questions in a realistic conversation.
