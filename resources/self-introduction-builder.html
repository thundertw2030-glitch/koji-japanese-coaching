PRACTICAL GUIDE

60-second Japanese Self-introduction Builder

Use this framework to draft a clear introduction for interviews or professional meetings.

1. Professional identity

“I am currently…” / “My main field is…”

2. Relevant experience

Choose one or two areas that connect directly to the listener.

3. Evidence-based strength

State a strength and add one short supporting example.

4. Connection

Explain why this conversation, role, or program is relevant.

Editing test

Remove any sentence that does not help the listener understand your fit, experience, or purpose. Then read the result aloud and adjust it to your natural speaking pace.

Need personal practice?

Use this framework as a starting point, then practice follow-up questions in a realistic conversation.
